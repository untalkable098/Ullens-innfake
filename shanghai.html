About Shanghai

Shanghai, on China's central coast, is the country's biggest city and a global financial hub. Its heart is the Bund, a famed waterfront promenade lined with colonial-era buildings. Across the Huangpu River rises the Pudong district’s futuristic skyline, including 632m Shanghai Tower and the Oriental Pearl TV Tower. Sprawling Yu Garden has traditional pavilions, towers, and ponds.

  • Metro population: 24,802,600
  • Mayor: Gong Zheng
  • Age of the city: About 734 years
  • Founded: 1291
  • Area: 6,340 km²
  • Elevation: 4 m
